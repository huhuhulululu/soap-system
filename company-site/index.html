How It Works · 工作流程

From Appointment to Payment
in Four Steps

从预约到收款，四步完成

Our automated pipeline handles the entire billing lifecycle. You see patients — we take care of the rest.

我们的自动化流水线处理整个账单生命周期。您负责看诊——其余的交给我们。

01

Data Collection 数据采集

We automatically pull appointments, patient data, and clinical notes from your EHR system daily.
每日自动从您的EHR系统提取预约、患者数据和临床记录。

02

Validation & Submission 验证与提交

Claims are validated through our 4-layer engine, then batch-submitted to clearinghouses with zero manual input.
索赔通过四层验证引擎校验后，批量提交至清算所，零人工干预。

03

Payment Tracking 付款追踪

ERA files are parsed and matched automatically. Denials are triaged and flagged for appeal or resubmission.
ERA文件自动解析匹配。拒赔自动分诊，标记申诉或重新提交。

04

Reconciliation 对账

Cross-system verification ensures every dollar is accounted for. Anomalies are detected and resolved proactively.
跨系统核验确保每一笔款项都有据可查。异常主动检测并解决。
